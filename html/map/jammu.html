Jammu and Kashmir

	
 Capital : Srinagar, Jammu   Language : Kashmiri, Dogri  Chief minister : Farooq Abdullah   Bird : Black-necked crane   Animal : Kashmir stag

Jammu and Kashmir is a state in northern India. It is located mostly in the Himalayan mountains. It shares a border with the states of Himachal Pradesh and Punjab to the south. Jammu and Kashmir have an international border with China in the north and east. The Line of Control separates it from the Pakistan-controlled territories of Azad Kashmir and Gilgit-Baltistan in the west and northwest respectively. The state has special autonomy under Article 370 of the Constitution of India.
